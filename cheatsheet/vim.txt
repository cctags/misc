# Vim cheat sheet

### Making a list of numbers

```
:put =range(11,15)
:put =map(range(1,90), 'printf(''[ ] %02d.'', v:val)')
:for i in range(1,10) | put ='192.168.0.'.i | endfor
```

[reference](http://vim.wikia.com/wiki/Making_a_list_of_numbers)
